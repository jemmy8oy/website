Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on the Ten Beer Plan app for iPhone and any other devices and platforms.

Information we collect

New Ten Beer Plan accounts are created via Sign In With Apple or Sign In With Google.

Apple Account ID and Google Account ID are stored only for logging in purposes.

We store the drinks that you log and any other post or profile information that you upload. This information is deleted when you delete your account.

Alcohol Disclaimer

We promote responsible drinking and encourage all users to consume alcohol in moderation.
  • Legal Drinking Age: Our products and services are intended for adults of legal drinking age.
  • Health & Safety: Excessive alcohol consumption can be harmful. Please drink responsibly.
  • No Drinking & Driving: Never drink and drive. Always arrange for a designated driver or use transportation services.
